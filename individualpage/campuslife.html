  • Campus Life at IIITDM Kancheepuram: A Vibrant Community of Learning and Fun

    IIITDM Kancheepuram is known not just for its excellent academic programs but also for its vibrant campus life. The institute offers a welcoming and inclusive environment where students can explore their interests, build lasting friendships, and have fun. The campus is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities including well-stocked libraries, modern labs, sports grounds, and comfortable hostels. Apart from academic pursuits, students can engage in a wide range of extracurricular activities such as music, dance, theater, and sports. There are also several clubs and societies catering to various interests such as coding, robotics, photography, and entrepreneurship. The institute hosts various cultural and technical fests throughout the year, providing students with ample opportunities to showcase their talents and learn from their peers. With its lively and enriching campus life, IIITDM Kancheepuram offers a holistic learning experience that goes beyond the classroom.
