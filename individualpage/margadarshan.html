Institute Newsletter

Marg Darshan

Margdarshan is the official newsletter of the Indian Institute of Information Technology, Design and Manufacturing Kancheepuram (IIITDM). It captures, in a highly readable format through its attractive blend of the visual and the textual, the multitude of activities that take place in the institute in its various disciplines and endeavours. Margdarshan is completely led and managed by the students of IIITDM. The reports and the images are enthusiastically contributed by the students, faculty and the very supportive administrative staff under the aegis of the dynamic director of the institute. The newsletter is highly newsworthy to the fraternities within the institute, the industry, the Government of India, other institutions of higher learning, and the wider public. Marg Darshan is a newsletter that leaves a very firm and attractive trail of the vibrant activities of IIITDM Kancheepuram.
