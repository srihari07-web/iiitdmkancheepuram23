  • 'Transforming Lives Together: IIITDM Kancheepuram Social Service Group'

    IIITDM Kancheepuram Social Service Group is a dynamic and dedicated community of individuals who are committed to making a positive impact on society. With a shared passion for service and a drive to create a better world, this group engages in various social initiatives that aim to uplift and empower the underprivileged.
